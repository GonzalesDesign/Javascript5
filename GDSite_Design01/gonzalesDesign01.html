Simply Beautiful. Let your ideas soar.

The simplicity of an origami is what makes it attractive and it’s also what makes it complicated. At first glance an origami is deceivingly simple until you start making one. It has to be scored at the right places and folded precisely at the right angles with patience, determination and fun doing it. Similarly to making a beautiful and functional website. It has to be crafted beautifully and precisely!

Portfolio. Traditional and Digital.

I always find myself making something out of nothing and it almost, always, start with pencil and paper. Designing print materials to screen media to building furniture and welding sculptures, they always start from a lead and paper. I enjoy working with both traditional and digital formats. There’s so much to do on both platforms. Knowing your tools and keeping abreast of them is a personal must for both traditional and digital tools.

My portfolio is presented here in digital format featuring my traditional works. Digitally the images are processed through Lightroom and Photoshop. Initial designs are done with pencil and paper, laid out on Sublime3 with emmet using html5, css3, javascript and bootstrap codes. Crafted with Media Queries to make sure it will display to most screen readers.
